Ko wai mātou | About This Course

This course uses Paulo Freire’s pedagogy of the oppressed — education is not a banking transaction but a dialogue. Each module begins with a generative theme drawn from real working-class experience in Aotearoa, followed by critical reflection and practical action steps (praxis).

All content is free, open-source, and published under Creative Commons. Built by The Kiwi Dialectic.
